About Me

Who Am I?

I should definitely find someone else to write this section. Does anyone like writing their own about me? I don't think so. My name is Conrad Southworth. I am 29 years old. I moved to Utah when I was 10 from Colorado. I grew up in a non-conventional Latter-day Saint family. My mother is a convert to the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and my father was born into it. I would say my family was less-active until moving to Utah. Growing up surrounded by other members of the church it was easy to be active and to participate in mutual and such.
